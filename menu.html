Our Menu

A Culinary Journey Through Ancient Yemen

Taste the Tradition

Select a category below to filter our dishes. Each dish is cooked fresh using heirloom spices and 100% Halal meats authorized by HFSAA.

Sufra Samosa

Appetizer

Golden, crispy triangular pastry pockets filled with a savory blend of spiced potatoes, green peas, onions, and local aromatic herbs. Served with mint chutney.

Chili Cheese Kulcha

Appetizer

Soft, tandoor-baked flatbread stuffed with molten cheddar cheese and finely chopped spicy green chilies. A savory, hot starter.

Chaat Medley

Appetizer

A vibrant mix of sweet potato cubes, crispy chickpea batter, tangy mint-tamarind yogurt, chopped onions, and fresh cilantro. Sweet, spicy, and refreshing.

Traditional Maraq

Soup

Clear lamb broth slow-simmered for hours with onions, cardamom, garlic, black pepper, and cumin. Garnished with fresh coriander and lime.

Sufra House Salad

Salad

Crisp romaine lettuce, vine-ripened tomatoes, crunchy English cucumbers, and red onions, tossed in a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing topped with sumac.

Signature Lamb Fahsa

Traditional Entrée

A sizzling shredded lamb stew slow-cooked in a traditional black stone pot (Hradah), topped with whipped green fenugreek froth (Hilbah) and served bubbling hot.

Chicken Saltah

Traditional Entrée

Yemeni national clay-pot stew featuring seasoned shredded chicken, mixed garden vegetables, and rich broth, capped with whipped hilbah.

Lamb Hanith

Lamb Specialty

Premium cut of young lamb seasoned with cardamon, cumin, and cloves, slow-roasted in specialized ovens for hours until meltingly tender, served on basmati rice.

Lamb Zurbian

Lamb Specialty

Fragrant rice dish cooked with slow-simmered tender lamb chunks, potatoes, saffron water, yogurt, and caramelized onions, infused with aromatic charcoal smoke.

Chicken Mandi

Chicken Dish

Half chicken seasoned with secret spices and roasted to crispy perfection, served on a bed of smoked basmati rice with a side of spicy Sahawek salsa.

Chicken Hanith

Chicken Dish

Slow-oven roasted chicken infused with garlic, cumin, and black pepper, roasted until tender, served with long-grain yellow rice.

Yemeni Baked Fish

Seafood

Premium fresh fish fillet marinated in a traditional tomato-garlic paste, red pepper, and cumin, baked in our clay oven to capture smoky wood flavors.

Vegetable Saltah

Vegetarian

A bubbling hot stone pot of mixed seasonal vegetables, okra, potatoes, and beans cooked in a seasoned broth, finished with whipped hilbah.

Foul Mudammas

Vegetarian

Slow-cooked premium fava beans mashed with sautéed onions, tomatoes, garlic, cumin, and olive oil, served with warm fresh bread.

Mulawah Flatbread

Fresh Bread

Giant, flaky, multi-layered traditional flatbread stretched by hand and baked fresh to order in our hot tandoor oven, brushed with light ghee.

Rashoush Bread

Fresh Bread

Traditional Yemeni whole wheat flatbread baked on hot stones, brushed with ghee, and sprinkled with black sesame seeds (Habbat al-Barakah).

Coconut Kheer

Dessert

Creamy, slow-cooked rice pudding infused with cardamon, saffron, whole milk, and shredded coconut, garnished with crushed pistachios.

Adeni Tea

Beverage

Traditional spiced milk tea brewed with black tea leaves, condensed milk, crushed cardamom pods, cloves, and ginger. Served hot.
