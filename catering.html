Our Catering Process

We ensure a smooth, worry-free process from the first consultation call to the final event setup.

1. Consultation

We discuss your event dates, venue location, guest counts, and initial cuisine preferences to outline basic requirements.

2. Custom Menu Planning

Our culinary specialists draft a tailored menu proposal, adjusting appetizer mixtures, mains, and desserts until it is perfect.

3. Gourmet Preparation

Our experienced chefs handcraft your feast on the event morning, utilizing strictly fresh ingredients and traditional slow-roasting ovens.

4. Delivery & Elegant Setup

Our team arrives at your venue, setting up the chaffing dishes, gold accents, and serving trays in a clean, visual arrangement.

5. Event Support

We can supply full-service server assistance during dinner, ensuring clean food replenishments, and handle cleanup at the end.
