Welcome to Sufra

A Feast of Ancient Heritage & Premium Flavors

At Sufra (which means "dining table" in Arabic), we welcome you into a culinary sanctuary where centuries of Yemeni tradition meet the comfort of contemporary hospitality. Nestled in Lombard, Illinois, we serve as the gateway to authentic Middle Eastern cuisine cooked to perfection.

Our experienced chefs are deeply passionate about preserving authentic Yemeni flavors. Utilizing premium ingredients, hand-slaughtered Halal meats authorized by the HFSAA, and ancestral slow-roasting techniques, each dish tells a story of craftsmanship, culture, and hospitality.

The Art of Slow Cooking

Savor the Legendary Hanith

Cooked for hours in specialized ovens until the lamb is fall-off-the-bone tender, our Signature Lamb Hanith is seasoned with custom cardamom and cumin spice blends. The result is a savory, aromatic masterclass in texture and flavor, served over a bed of smoked basmati rice.
